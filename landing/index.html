FAQ

Common questions

How does pricing work?

Sophikon is free for individuals and small teams. Pro and Enterprise plans are available for larger organizations with advanced resource management, SSO, and priority support.

Can I import from MS Project or other tools?

Yes. Sophikon supports XML and MPP import and export, compatible with MS Project, Primavera P6, and other standards-based tools. Migration takes seconds.

Is my data secure?

All data is hosted on EU infrastructure and is GDPR-compliant by design. You can also self-host Sophikon on your own servers for complete control.

How long does it take to get started?

Most teams are up and running within minutes. Import your existing projects, invite your team, and start managing — no training required.
